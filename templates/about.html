About OptiCrop

Empowering farmers with data-driven decisions for a sustainable future.

OptiCrop is an AI-powered agricultural recommendation system designed to eliminate the guesswork from farming. By analyzing crucial soil nutrients (Nitrogen, Phosphorous, Potassium), soil pH, and environmental factors (Temperature, Humidity, Rainfall), our Machine Learning engine predicts the absolute best crop for your specific plot of land.

Built as a robust end-to-end Machine Learning pipeline, OptiCrop achieves over 99% accuracy by utilizing a highly tuned Random Forest algorithm.
